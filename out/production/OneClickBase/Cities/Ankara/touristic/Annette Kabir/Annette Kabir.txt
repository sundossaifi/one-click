name : Anit Kabir
location : Cankaya/Ankara
desc : Anit Kabir ("monument" in Turkish) is the mausoleum of Mustafa Kemal Ataturk. The mausoleum is located in the capital, Ankara, and was designed by architects Emin Onat and Ahmet Orhan Arda, whose design beat 48 other entries from several countries, in a competition led by the Turkish government in 1941.
